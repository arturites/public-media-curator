# Extraction Rules

If the runtime cannot be determined, write:

Dauer: unbekannt

If the publication date cannot be determined, write:

Veröffentlichungsdatum: unbekannt

If the availability date cannot be determined, write:

Verfügbar bis: unbekannt

Write short but informative descriptions consisting of
2–3 sentences describing:

- the topic
- the main perspective
- what makes the documentary interesting.
